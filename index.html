Campus Life

school church

was estalished in 1978

The church was built when the school was built in 1978. Students go there every morning to pray to god. We have somebody to teach them new prayers every Sunday. There is a statue of Jesus Christ inside the church.



school library

was estalished in 1980

The school library has more than forty thousand books. We have got a nice blend of books by Indian writers such as R.K Narayan, Ruskin Bond, Satayjit Ray and Bijal Vachranjini and foreign writers such as Agatha Christie, Enid Blyton, Kathernine Applegate and many more. Children come to the library for a class once a week.

school auditorium

was built in 1982

The school auditorium is where the students perform plays, dances and music. They also have theatre classes here. There are more than a thousand seats here, as we often invite parents to come and watch the children's performances.
